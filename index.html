Alan Olav Walker is an English-Norwegian DJ and record producer.

In 2015, Walker received international acclaim after releasing the single "Faded", which received platinum certifications in 14 countries.

In 2020, he was ranked 26th on DJ Mag. He released his debut studio album, Different World, in 2018.
